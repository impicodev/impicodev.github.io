War Engine Battlegrounds

An action-packed game where players command a heavily armed train hurtling through a post-apocalyptic wasteland. As the train conductor, you have the power to upgrade, add, and swap weapon modules to fend off hordes of enemies and colossal bosses that stand in your way. Along the railway tracks, strategically placed checkpoints and shops provide opportunities to repair and enhance your train's defenses.

Your skill in maneuvering the train and optimizing your weapon loadout becomes crucial for survival. The modular nature of your train allows you to experiment with different weapon combinations, selecting devastating artillery cannons, flamethrowers, or electrifying Tesla coils to annihilate your foes.

Wordle Solver

Wordle Solver 1.0:
A console based program that takes your wordle guesses, does some calculations, and recommends a new guess. The algorithm filters out words that don't match the criteria and evaluates remaining words based on letter frequencies in the english language.

Wordle Solver 2.0 (shown here):
A fully automatic program, powered by version 1.0, that will make the guesses for you. It simulates key presses, reads the pixel colors from the screen, runs version 1.0 on the current state, and repeats.
